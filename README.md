**The lightweight bridge between your browser and Discord.**

BrowserCord automatically updates your Discord "Playing" status based on what you are watching or browsing. Whether it's YouTube, Twitch, or other supported platforms, let your friends see what you're up to without lifting a finger.

## üöÄ Quick Start

1. **Download**: Grab the latest `.exe` (Server) and `.zip` (Extension) from [Releases](https://github.com/JTDMedia/browsercord/releases).
2. **Run the Server**: Open `BrowserCord` on your desktop.
3. **Install Extension**: Load the extension into your browser.
4. **Connect**: Open Discord, click the extension icon, and ensure it says **"Connected"**.

> For detailed instructions, check out the **[Full Installation Guide](https://github.com/JTDMedia/browsercord/wiki#%EF%B8%8F-how-to-install)**.
